Line break tags are used to bring cursor to the next line.

They are generally used within the paragraph tags to bring the rest of the content of the paragraph to the next line.

The difference between paragraph tag and line break tag is that paragraph tag brings the cursor to the next next line while line break tag brings the cursor to just to the next line.
